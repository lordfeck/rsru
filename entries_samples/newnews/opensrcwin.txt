The collected essays of Richard Stallman are said to have swayed Redmond's C-suite. This prompted much soul-searching over at the Microsoft HQ and the codebase was released to the public eye, following the recent example of Twitter.<br><br>
GNOME 3 users are eagerly awaiting a port of Windows Explorer to Linux, it is also reported. This means they will finally experience a UI that has better coherency than they've ever known before!
